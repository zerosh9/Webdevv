The Kamikaze Ride

The Kamikaze Ride is an intense amusement park attraction featuring two swinging pendulum arms with spinning gondolas. Riders experience thrilling swings and spins, creating a heart-pounding adventure for those seeking an adrenaline rush.

Roller Coaster

Our roller coaster ride is a wild journey on a track filled with twists, turns, and sudden drops. It's a thrilling, high-speed experience that combines excitement and gravity-defying moments, offering an exhilarating adventure for thrill-seekers.

Ferris Wheel

Experience the magic of our Ferris Wheel! Our gentle, circular ride invites you to soar to new heights in comfortable gondolas, treating you to breathtaking panoramic views of the park and beyond.

Flying Chair

Feel the thrill as you board your own flying chair, suspended in the air, and let the excitement take flight. Soar through the skies and enjoy the sensation of weightlessness as you spin and twirl high above the park.
